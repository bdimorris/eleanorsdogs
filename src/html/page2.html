Retrievers & Labradors

Retrievers are lively, energetic, and like to play around. They are a very popular family dog, but can also work - being used to help with hunting, as guide dogs for the blind, and lots of other things.


Characteristics

Labrador retrievers are short-haired, while normal retrievers are slightly longer-haired. They come in lots of colours, ranging from black, through chocolate brown, to golden yellow.
